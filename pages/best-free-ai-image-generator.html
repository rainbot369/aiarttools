What to Look For

  1. Daily/weekly limits — how many images can you actually create?
  2. Watermarks — does the free plan add visible watermarks?
  3. Model access — are you limited to lower-quality models?
  4. Commercial rights — can you use free-tier images commercially?
  5. Feature restrictions — which features are locked behind paywalls?
