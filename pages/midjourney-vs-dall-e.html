Midjourney Overview

Midjourney produces the most visually stunning AI images available today. Its V6.1 model excels at artistic compositions, lighting, and style consistency. The Discord community provides endless inspiration, and the parameter system (--style, --chaos, --stylize) gives experienced users fine-grained control over output.

DALL-E 3 Overview

DALL-E 3 through ChatGPT is the most accessible AI image generator. Simply describe what you want in plain English, and ChatGPT translates your request into optimized prompts. The conversational editing flow — asking for specific changes in follow-up messages — makes iterating effortless.
