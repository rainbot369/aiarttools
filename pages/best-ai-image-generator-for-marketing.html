What to Look For

  1. Speed of iteration — can you quickly generate multiple variations for A/B testing?
  2. Brand consistency — does the tool support style matching or presets?
  3. Commercial rights — are generated images safe to use in ads and campaigns?
  4. Team collaboration — can multiple team members access the tool?
  5. Integration — does it connect with your existing design tools?
